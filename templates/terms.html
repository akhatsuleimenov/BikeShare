Eligibility

The Program is available only to students, faculty, and staff of New York University Abu Dhabi who are in good standing and possess a valid NYUAD ID card.

General Use

Users are expected to follow all traffic laws and regulations while using the bikes. Bikes should be used responsibly and returned to designated bike stations in good condition.

Safety

All users are encouraged to wear helmets at all times while riding. The Program does not provide helmets, and users must procure their own.

Prohibited Activities

Bikes may not be used for racing, mountain biking, stunt riding, or any other use outside of normal transportation purposes. Users found misusing bikes will face penalties including but not limited to suspension from the Program.

Fees and Penalties

There may be fees associated with the use of the Program. Late returns, damage, or loss of bikes will incur additional fees and penalties.
